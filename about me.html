Most famous Signers

John Hancock
He was the president of the Continental Congress and is known for his bold and prominent signature, which became a symbol of American independence.
Thomas Jefferson
The principal author of the Declaration of Independence, Jefferson's ideas and writing helped shape the document.
Benjamin Franklin
A statesman, inventor, and diplomat, Franklin was one of the most respected figures in America and Europe at the time.
John Adams
A strong advocate for independence and a future president of the United States, Adams was a key figure in pushing for separation from Britain.
Samuel Adams
A leader in the American Revolution and cousin to John Adams, Samuel was one of the leading figures in advocating for independence in Massachusetts.
Richard Henry Lee
He was the one who proposed the resolution for independence to the Continental Congress, which led to the Declaration itself.
